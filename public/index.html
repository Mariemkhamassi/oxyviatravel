Tour Plan

Welcome to Sidi Bou Saïd, where charm meets history

Day 1

Morning – SIDI BOU SAÏD: Welcome to a dream of blue and white. Settle into Dar Fatma, a hidden gem overlooking the shimmering Gulf of Tunis.

Lunch – SIDI BOU SAÏD: A feast of flavors awaits—FRESH SALADS, FRAGRANT SOUP, & COUSCOUS, kissed by the spices of tradition. End with SEASONAL FRUITS, nature’s sweet delight.

Afternoon – SIDI BOU SAÏD: Wander the poetic streets, where bougainvillea spills over whitewashed walls. Sip MINT TEA at Café des Délices, savoring a golden BAMBALOUNI, crisp and sweet.

Late Afternoon – CARTHAGE: Step into history’s embrace. Among UNESCO-listed ruins, hear whispers of Phoenician legends and Roman conquests. Time stands still in the echoes of an empire.

Evening – DAR FATMA: Dine under a sky of quiet stars. Let local flavors dance on your palate as you gaze upon the Gulf’s golden shimmer. The magic of Tunisia has only just begun.

Between the captivating scents of the Medina and cultural immersion in Kairouan.

Day 2

Morning – TUNIS MEDINA: Step into a maze of history, where spice-laden air and leather scents awaken your senses. At El Zittouna, craft your own fragrance, blending tradition with creativity.

Lunch – TUNIS MEDINA: Indulge in a refined meal at DOKEN, a hidden gem where Tunisian and Mediterranean flavors unite in a culinary masterpiece.

Afternoon – KAIROUAN: Travel to Tunisia’s spiritual heart. Wander through the Great Mosque of Okba, a UNESCO treasure echoing centuries of devotion.

Evening – KAIROUAN: Settle into your hotel for a feast of local flavors. Then, hands-on tradition—craft MAKROUDH, Kairouan’s iconic pastry, steeped in history and honeyed delight.

A Feast for the Senses – History & Flavors

Day 3

Morning – EL DJEM: Roam the grand amphitheater, where echoes of gladiators still linger. Admire intricate mosaics before diving into the fragrant souks.

Lunch – EL DJEM: Feast on a regal COUSCOUS, kissed by spices, followed by a golden HONEY & ALMOND CAKE, the sweet essence of tradition.

Afternoon – SFAX: Wander the old city, where history meets the sea breeze. Taste Tunisia’s treasure—EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL, liquid gold in every drop.

Evening – KERKENNAH: As the sky melts into the sea, savor FRESH SEAFOOD, a gift of the waves, in a tranquil island setting.

The Soul of the Sea – Fishing & Folklore

Day 4

Morning – KERKENNAH: Drift into the dawn aboard a FLOUKA, where fishermen unveil the secrets of CHARFIYA, a dance between sea and net. Savor the ocean’s gift, a FRESH CATCH, grilled to perfection..

Afternoon – KERKENNAH: Cycle through whispering palms, past the glistening SALT FLATS, where the land meets the sky in a mirrored dream.

Evening – KERKENNAH: Dine beneath the stars, feasting on SEAFOOD DELICACIES, ,before losing yourself in the rhythm of a FOLKLORE SPECTACLE.

Desert Jewels – Oasis & Underground Wonders

Day 5

Morning – SFAX TO GABÈS: Journey to Gabès, where palms sway over ancient trade routes. Sip LOCAL JUICE, taste the sun-baked crunch of KHOBZA M’TABGHA, and let the medina’s scents enchant you.

Lunch – CHENNINI GABÈS: Lunch is Served in the heart of an oasis, savoring LAMB COUSCOUS, KEFTA, OR MOLOUKHIA, followed by steaming MINT TEA, sweet as desert whispers.

Afternoon – MATMATA: Step into a land of legends, where troglodyte homes carve poetry into the mountains, and sunsets paint the sky in fire and gold.

Evening – MATMATA: Stir ancient flavors in a COOKING WORKSHOP, crafting M’HASSA,KISSRA & TRUFFLES, then feast beneath a canopy of stars.

From Matmata to Djerba – Culture, Cinema & Traditions

Day 6

Morning – MATMATA: Explore ancient troglodyte homes and step into the world of Star Wars at Anakin Skywalker’s house, where cinema meets tradition.

Midday – KSAR GHILANE: Journey to a golden oasis. Ride a quad or camel across the dunes or unwind in a natural hot spring.

Lunch – KSAR GHILANE: Savor a feast of tender lamb, fresh salads, and fragrant tea, a taste of desert hospitality.

Afternoon – DJERBA: Travel to Houmt Souk, where history and island charm await.

A Symphony of Flavors & Traditions

Day 7

Morning – DJERBA: Step into a local kitchen, hands dusted with spices, hearts warmed by stories, as you prepare TRADITIONAL DISHES with a Djerbian family.

Afternoon – GUELLALA:n the hands of artisans, clay spins into timeless treasures. Wander through the POTTERY VILLAGE, then refresh with a golden, crispy BRIK.

Evening – DJERBA: Beneath lantern light, dine on ISLAND SPECIALTIES,Then dance to the heartbeat of Djerba in a mesmerizing FOLK PERFORMANCE.

A Farewell Wrapped in Sunlight

Day 8

Morning – HOUMT SOUK: Let the charm of whitewashed alleys and bustling markets embrace you one last time. Sip, taste, wander—soak in the magic.

Lunch – DJERBA:Savor your final meal, a plate of COUSCOUS DJERBIENor a tender GRILLED FISH, infused with the essence of the Mediterranean.

Afternoon – DJERBA ZARZIS AIRPORT: Carry with you the flavors, the rhythms, the warmth of Tunisia—a journey that lingers long after you’ve left its shores.
